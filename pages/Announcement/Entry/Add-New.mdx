# Add New Announcement

You can add announcements easily using UdyogX.

## Stepwise Guide

1. **Log in** to the software by entering your **username** and **password**.
2. In the **left sidebar menu**, go to **`Announcement > Add New`**.
![Alt Text](https://cyberpassion.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/Screenshot-2024-09-15-at-5.21.42-PM.png)

3. A form will open with the following fields:
   - **Category**: Select the appropriate category for the announcement.
   - **Date**: Choose the date for the announcement.
   - **Content**: Enter the content of the announcement.
4. On the right side of the form, choose the **recipient** from the menu.
5. After filling out the form, click **`Save`**.
6. Upon successful submission, a **confirmation modal** will appear to confirm that the announcement has been added.
