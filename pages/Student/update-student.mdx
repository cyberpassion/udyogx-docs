Log in to the software.

1. Go to the “Student” module and click on “View Student List.”
2. In the list, find the student’s name, and click on the filter arrow located next to it.
3. Select the option “Update” from the dropdown menu.
4. In the “Update” section, make the necessary changes to the student’s name or any other details.
5. After making the changes, click on “Save” to update the student’s details.

By following these steps, you can successfully update student details in the software.
