# Creating UdyogX Account

To create your UdyogX account follow the steps,

1. Visit marketplace.udyogx.in and selected your desired plan.
2. Install the plan and your will recieve a confirmation on email.
3. Login to your account and do basic confirguration like adding business name, phone number, admin details and more.
4. Once set, you are now ready to start using UdyogX.
